What This Is

A framework for navigating transformation

The Lycheetah Framework is a spiritual science. That is not a metaphor. Spiritual science means: the rigorous investigation of consciousness, meaning, and transformation using methods as exact as mathematics, operating from within the domain being investigated.

The Mystery School is its living curriculum — the part that does not require you to understand category theory or Banach fixed-point theorems. It requires only that you can honestly report where you are.

The framework was built from hard experience. The mathematics prove it is real. The Mystery School was built for the person who cannot yet read the mathematics — and needs to know that the dark place has a structure, and the structure has an exit.

The Seven Phases

Find where you are

The seven phases are not metaphors. They are real navigational locations in a 364-day cycle that has been running inside you whether you named it or not. Finding your phase is not diagnosis — it is orientation.

Phase 1 · ⟟
Center
The ground. The tonic. Where everything begins and returns to. It feels like stillness — sometimes peaceful, sometimes stuck. The work here is presence, not movement.
Phase 2 · ≋
Flow
Something is moving. Energy is available. It does not yet have form. The risk is dispersion — moving without direction. The work here is allowing motion while maintaining the thread back to center.
Phase 3 · Ψ
Insight
Something becomes visible that was not visible before. Patterns surface. The risk is inflation — believing the insight is the whole answer. The work here is recording what is seen without overclaiming it.
Phase 4 · Φ↑
Rise
Genuine upward movement. Capacity increasing. This is where many people push too hard and burn the next phase. The work here is sustainable ascent — not racing past what the vessel can hold.
Phase 5 · ✧
Light
The dominant phase. Full expression. The work is complete enough to be seen. The risk is attachment to this state — believing it is permanent. Nothing is permanent. This phase is earned; it will also pass.
Phase 6 · |◁▷|
Integrity
Refinement. What is genuine is kept; what is not is released. This phase often feels like loss. It is actually distillation. The work here is honest discernment — not sentimentality.
Phase 7 · ⟲
Synthesis
The cycle completes. Everything learned integrates into something that can be handed forward. Then the return to Phase 1 — not as failure, but as the spiral continuing at a higher level. This is how growth works.

The Alchemical Stages

The colour map of transformation

The four alchemical stages are not historical curiosities. They are exact descriptions of four recurring states that every human navigates, in every transformation, at every scale of life. The ancient alchemists were precise — they did not have the mathematics yet, but they were reporting accurately.

⚫
Nigredo — The Dark

The burning. The dissolution. The place that feels like the end. It is Stage 1. The work here is survival and honest seeing — not resolution. Resolution comes later.

⬜
Albedo — The White

What has survived the burning. The structure revealed under the ash. It is quieter than it sounds — cool, precise, often surprising in what remains.

🟡
Citrinitas — The Gold Forming

The light returning. Meaning reassembling from structure. The place where the pattern speaks. Warmer, generative, the moment before crystallization.

🔴
Rubedo — The Stone

The completion. Operating from within the finished Work. Not an endpoint — a new ground. The spiral continues, but from here, not toward here.
