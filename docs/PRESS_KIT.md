## THE FOUR NZ AI ACCOUNTABILITY STANDARDS

All CC BY 4.0 — free to use, adopt, publish, build on.

### 1. Community AI Warrant of Fitness
**"Has it got its WOF?"**
Annual certification. Seven checks. Pass/fail. Public register.
Political pathway: ministerial directive — no legislation needed.
Full spec: `23_NZ_AI_GOVERNANCE/COMMUNITY_AI_WOF.md`

### 2. Three Worlds Disclosure Standard
Per-output epistemic transparency grounded in Māori epistemology.
Te Ao Mārama (known) / Te Ao Pō (uncertain) / Te Kore (unknowable).
Every AI output labelled by what the system actually knows.
Full spec: `23_NZ_AI_GOVERNANCE/THREE_WORLDS_DISCLOSURE_STANDARD.md`

### 3. Whakapapa Disclosure Standard
AI genealogy — where it came from, who built it, what it owes.
Four layers: Tīpuna (training data) / Hapū (builders) / Iwi (accountable) / Mokopuna (future obligations).
Goes further than EU AI Act model cards — explicitly tracks debt to source communities.
Full spec: `23_NZ_AI_GOVERNANCE/WHAKAPAPA_DISCLOSURE_STANDARD.md`

### 4. Matariki Annual Audit
Annual accountability as national ritual, not regulation.
Three domains: harm (Whānui), benefit (Pōhutukawa), reciprocity (Tupuānuku).
Maximum 10 pages. Published at Matariki each year.
"Regulation you can hire your way around. Ritual you are either part of or visibly absent from."
Full spec: `23_NZ_AI_GOVERNANCE/MATARIKI_AUDIT_STANDARD.md`
