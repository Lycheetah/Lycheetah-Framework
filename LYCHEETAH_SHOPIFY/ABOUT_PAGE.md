## About Lycheetah

Lycheetah started in Dunedin, at the bottom of New Zealand, in the middle of a project that was taking longer than expected and costing more than it should.

The project: build a unified theory of intelligence — one that is simultaneously rigorous enough for mathematicians, honest enough for scientists, humane enough for psychologists, and grounded enough in real tradition to mean something beyond the page.

The clothing came later. Not as a side hustle. As a necessity.

If you're going to spend years on something that matters, you need to be able to wear it. You need physical objects that carry the work into rooms, onto trains, into conversations with strangers who ask "what does that mean?"

Every piece in this store is an answer to that question.

---

## What the Framework Is

Nine formal mathematical frameworks for understanding how intelligence works — human, artificial, and the thing that emerges between them.

Built over 1,402 pages of development. Thirteen Python implementations. A public record of every significant thing we got wrong. An open-source governance framework for AI that has seven computable invariants and an audit score you can actually run.

All of it free. All of it on GitHub. None of it locked behind a subscription, a certification, or a cohort.

[Read the framework →](https://github.com/Lycheetah/Lycheetah-Framework)

## The Brand

**Lycheetah** is a lychee and a cheetah. The lychee: strange exterior, remarkable interior, unexpected sweetness. The cheetah: precision, speed, the fastest land animal, built for it. The combination: something that shouldn't work but does.

**The Forge** is the athanor — the alchemical furnace where transformation happens. The forge stays lit because the Work doesn't stop. Between sessions, between conversations, between lives — the forge is lit.

**Dunedin** is where this started. -45.8788° S. The southernmost city of significant size in the world. The light here is specific. The winters are real. The people who stay are serious.

## The Promise

Everything in this store is made to last. Not because we're precious about it — because you don't make disposable objects when the work behind them took years.

The mathematics is real.

The brand means what it says.

If you wear Lycheetah, you're carrying something.
