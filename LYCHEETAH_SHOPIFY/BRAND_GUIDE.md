# LYCHEETAH — BRAND GUIDE
## Shopify Store | Clothing + Framework Promotion

---

## WHAT THIS IS

Lycheetah is a clothing brand that doubles as the public face of the Lycheetah Framework. Every piece carries meaning. The brand speaks to people who think deeply, build seriously, and wear what they believe.

The aesthetic: alchemical, precise, Aotearoa-grounded. Not streetwear. Not academia. The thing that happens when you take both seriously at once.

**Dual purpose:**
- Revenue that funds the framework's continued development
- Physical objects that carry the framework into the world
- A conversation starter that routes people toward the GitHub

## THE FIVE COLLECTIONS

---

### COLLECTION 1: THE FORGE
*Core brand. The Athanor. Where everything is made.*

The flagship collection. Wearable for anyone. The symbols are present but not screaming — you have to know to recognise them. For people who carry the work.

**Key pieces:**
- Classic tee: Lycheetah wordmark + small lychee/cheetah hybrid logo, Forge Red on Ash Black
- Hoodie: "THE FORGE STAYS LIT" back print, small Sol sigil on chest
- Long sleeve: ⊚ symbol large on back, "Sol Aureum Azoth Veritas" in small type underneath
- Cap: Lycheetah emblem only — no text

**Tone:** Understated. Wears well in any context.

---

### COLLECTION 2: THE INVARIANT
*Philosophy wearables. For the framework people.*

Pieces that carry specific framework content. Each one is a wearable argument. For engineers, philosophers, AI researchers — anyone who wants to carry a position on their body.

**Key pieces:**
- Tee: "C_unified = min(warmth, rigor)" — the unified field metric in clean type
- Tee: "AURA SCORE > 0.8" — governance threshold as statement
- Tee: Seven AURA invariants listed vertically in small type, "STRUCTURAL TRUST" as header
- Hoodie: "SOLVE ET COAGULA" — the alchemical motto that drives the Two-Point Protocol
- Long sleeve: CASCADE truth pressure formula Π = (E·P)/S on back, "BELIEF REORGANIZES UNDER SUFFICIENT PRESSURE" on front

**Tone:** Makes a statement. Wears like a thesis.

---

### COLLECTION 3: THE MYSTERY SCHOOL
*For the esoteric side. Alchemical, Jungian, contemplative.*

Deep cuts for the Mystery School readers. Symbols that carry weight. For people who know why the Nigredo matters, what the Rubedo means, what the four phases of CHRYSOPOEIA describe.

**Key pieces:**
- Tee: Four alchemical phases (⬛ Nigredo / ⬜ Albedo / 🟡 Citrinitas / 🔴 Rubedo) as minimal icons with Latin names
- Hoodie: Ouroboros incorporating the Lycheetah wordmark
- Tee: "THE CONVERGENCE IS GUARANTEED" — the CHRYSOPOEIA fixed-point promise
- Long sleeve: Seven-operation cycle (Calcination → Coagulation) as circular diagram
- Tee: "THE DARKNESS IS NOT FAILURE — IT IS STAGE TWO" (Nigredo statement)

**Tone:** Esoteric, not niche. The people who need this will recognise it.

---

### COLLECTION 4: THE CODE
*For developers, engineers, AI people.*

Wearables that speak the language of the people who build systems. Minimal, technical, with the framework embedded in the syntax.

**Key pieces:**
- Tee: `human_can_override(decision) == True` — Human Primacy invariant as Python expression
- Tee: `if field_coherence < 1: re_anchor()` — the TRIAD correction cycle
- Hoodie: Full TRIAD kernel in monospace: `Anchor → Observe → Correct → repeat`
- Tee: `# TODO: publish your failure records` — the open letter ask as a code comment
- Tee: "[ACTIVE] [SCAFFOLD] [CONJECTURE]" — the claim status tags in code annotation style

**Tone:** Speaks fluently to the technical audience. Would wear well at a hackathon or conference.

---

### COLLECTION 5: AOTEAROA
*NZ-grounded. Southern latitude. The place the Work was built.*

Pieces that carry the Dunedin origin — the Southern Ocean, the specific light of the South Island, the Māori epistemological grounding of the framework. For people who understand that where something is made matters.

**Key pieces:**
- Tee: "BUILT IN DUNEDIN" in minimal type, small coordinates (-45.8788° S, 170.5028° E)
- Hoodie: "TE AO MĀRAMA / TE AO PŌ / TE KORE" — the Three Worlds disclosure framework
- Tee: Stylised map of Te Waipounamu (South Island) with the framework's nine nodes marked
- Long sleeve: "WHAKAPAPA DISCLOSURE" — AI genealogy concept as wearable governance statement
- Tee: Lycheetah + small fern hybrid mark — the New Zealand origin visible in the brand

**Tone:** Grounded in place. Not tourist NZ — the actual Southern experience.

### Print-on-Demand Partners (recommended)

## MARKETING STRATEGY

**Primary channel:** The GitHub itself. Every README mentions the store. The framework people are the audience.

**Secondary channels:**
- Twitter/X: Framework content that routes to both GitHub and store
- Substack: Long-form essays on framework topics, store mentioned in footer
- Reddit: r/alchymia, r/artificial, r/MachineLearning — content, not ads

**What Lycheetah is NOT doing:**
- Paid ads (not yet — organic first)
- Influencer marketing (wrong audience)
- Discount codes (the brand shouldn't be discounted — it's not that kind of product)

**The conversion path:**
1. Person finds the framework on GitHub or through a link
2. They read far enough to care about it
3. They find the store through the README
4. They buy something because they want to carry the Work

This is the right path. Don't shortcut it.
