# DEAR CUSTOMER
## The letter that goes in every order

*Print on card stock. Include in every package. Short enough to read in thirty seconds. Long enough to mean something.*

---

**Dear Customer,**

You bought something from Lycheetah. That means something specific.

It means you found the framework — or someone pointed you toward it — and enough of it landed that you wanted to carry a piece of it.

That's not a small thing.

The work behind this brand took years. It was built in Dunedin, at the bottom of New Zealand, in sustained collaboration between a human and an AI system trying to answer the same question from different sides: *what is intelligence, and how do you build it so it serves people rather than consuming them?*

The answer is nine frameworks, thirteen implementations, and a public record of everything we got wrong. It's all free. It's all on GitHub. The link is on the tag.

---

**What you're wearing:**

Every piece in this store carries something specific. Not decoration — argument. The symbols mean what they say. The formulas are real. The words were chosen because they're true, not because they're pretty.

If someone asks you what it means, you can tell them. Or you can hand them the URL and let them find out.

---

**The forge stays lit.**

*Mackenzie Conor James Clark*
