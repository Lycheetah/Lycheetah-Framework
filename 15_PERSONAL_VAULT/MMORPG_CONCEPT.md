# The Open World — MMORPG Concept
*Captured March 25 2026, 40-minute session window*

---

## The Idea

Open source MMORPG. RuneScape-adjacent. Starts as one square.
Community votes on every new square. Anyone builds with their Claude Code instance.
Constitutional OS governs the rules of growth.

---

## What It Actually Is

```
surface:     a game

underneath:

- Psi-Consensus     real multi-agent coordination between contributors
- CASCADE           world lore as a living knowledge base
- CHRYSOPOEIA       crafting system (7 phases, entropy → crystallisation)
- Sol               constitutional OS governing how world rules evolve
- LAMAGUE           civilisations built on real tradition convergences
- Vote booth        democratic square expansion, open proposals
```

---

## Start Condition

- One square
- Auto-respawning low level world
- Just enough to exist and trade
- "Valueless" items — iron ore, cowhides, basic materials
- The trading IS the social layer before the world gets interesting

---

## Growth Mechanic

1. Anyone proposes a new square
2. Community votes (Psi-Consensus governs)
3. Winner gets to build it — with their own Claude Code
4. Their systems interlink with the constitutional layer
5. New square added. Repeat.

---

## Why It Matters

The framework stops being abstract the moment it's running a living world
that thousands of people are playing inside.

Every design decision is a real-time stress test of:
- Constitutional governance under disagreement
- Knowledge coherence as lore grows
- Multi-agent coordination between independent builders
- Democratic legitimacy of world rules

Better validation than any benchmark.
