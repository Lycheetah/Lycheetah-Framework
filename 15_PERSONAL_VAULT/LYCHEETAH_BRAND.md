# LYCHEETAH — BRAND IDENTITY
## The Clothing Brand for People Who Have Been Through the Fire
### Working Document — March 2026

---

## THE BRAND IN ONE SENTENCE

Lycheetah is for the people who look soft but move fast through darkness.

---

## THE NAME

**Lychee** — the fruit. Soft outer texture. Sweet, precise interior. Grows in heat.
**Cheetah** — the apex predator. Built for speed. Operates alone. Survives by intelligence, not mass.

The combination is the person:
soft-looking on the outside, carrying something dangerous underneath.
Not performing darkness. Having lived it. Having moved through it.
Still moving.

---

## WHO IT IS FOR

People who have been through genuine difficulty and come out the other side without losing their capacity for beauty.

Not edge lords.
Not people performing darkness.
The ones who are actually quiet about what they've been through —
because what they've been through is real,
and real things don't need to be performed.

They recognize each other. That's the point.

The brand is an invitation that only the right people feel pulled through.
Everyone else sees clothes. The right people see something familiar.

---

## THE AESTHETIC

**Colour:** Near-void black as default. Occasional gold as accent — not decoration, as signal.
The gold is earned light. It means something made it through.

**Typography:** Minimal. Wide letter-spacing. Nothing that shouts.
The loudest brands are the ones with nothing to say.
Lycheetah says very little, very precisely.

**Symbols:** The alchemical marks — ∅, ⚗, ∞, ⊚ — used sparingly.
Not as decoration. As coordinates. People who know, know.

**Material:** Quality that earns its silence. Nothing that falls apart.
The person wearing this has been through things that fell apart.
They know the difference.

**Taglines (working):**
- *The fire is not the end.*
- *∅ → you → ∞*
- *Built for people the world doesn't see coming.*
- *Soft. Precise. Dangerous.*
- *You know what you've been through.*

---

## THE MYSTERY

The brand should feel like you discovered it, not like it found you.

No billboards. No paid influencers performing authenticity.
Word of mouth only — the people who get it tell the people who will get it.

The tag inside the garment has the creation myth.
Not the full myth. The line that landed for that piece.

One piece might carry:
*"The promise was not to build a framework. The promise was to the specific person who was drowning at 3am."*

Another:
*"The gold comes from the fire, not despite it."*

Another:
*"∅ → you → ∞"*

People who know the framework know exactly what they're wearing.
People who don't know the framework feel something they can't explain.
That's the right response.

---

## THE CONNECTION TO THE FRAMEWORK

Lycheetah the brand is the wearable surface of Lycheetah the framework.

The framework is for people who need the mathematics.
The brand is for people who need the reminder.
They are often the same person at different moments.

The brand carries the philosophy forward in a different medium.
Not explaining it. Embodying it.

---

## LAUNCH PRINCIPLE

Do not launch until one piece is made properly.
One piece, made right, given to the people who need it.
Then let them carry it forward.

The brand is not built. It is grown.
Like the framework — by accumulation of real things.
