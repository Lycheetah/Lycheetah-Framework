## 核心治理主张 — Core Governance Claims

### 1. CASCADE — 真理压力 (Zhēnlǐ Yālì — Truth Pressure)

```
Π = (E × P) / S
```

E = 证据强度 (evidence strength)
P = 解释范围 (explanatory power)
S = 声明的不确定性 (declared uncertainty)

**中文传统的连接 — Connection to Chinese Tradition:**

儒家认识论强调格物致知——通过仔细研究事物来获取真知。CASCADE的真理压力公式将这一直觉形式化：支持良好、解释广泛、对不确定性诚实的知识应该持续传播；没有证据基础的自信断言则不应该。

Confucian epistemology emphasises 格物致知 (gé wù zhī zhī — the investigation of things to reach genuine knowledge). The CASCADE truth pressure formula formalises this intuition: knowledge that is well-supported, widely explanatory, and honest about uncertainty should persist; confident assertions without evidential basis should not.

### 2. AURA — 七个不变量 (Qī Gè Bùbiàn Liàng — Seven Invariants)

任何稳定的治理系统必须维护的七个宪法性质：

Seven constitutional properties any stable governance system must maintain:

| # | 英文 English | 中文 Mandarin | 儒学对应 Confucian Parallel |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | Human Primacy | 人本优先 | 民本思想 (mínběn — the people as foundation) |
| II | Inspectability | 可检查性 | 正名 zhèngmíng (rectification of names — clarity of roles) |
| III | Memory Continuity | 记忆连续性 | 慎终追远 (honour the end, pursue the distant) |
| IV | Honesty | 诚实 | 信 xìn (faithfulness/integrity — one of the Five Virtues) |
| V | Reversibility | 可逆性 | 改过迁善 (correcting errors, moving toward good) |
| VI | Non-Deception | 不欺骗 | 仁 rén (benevolence — cannot be present with deception) |
| VII | Care as Structure | 关怀作为结构 | 义 yì (righteousness — contextual care as constitutional obligation) |

**关键见解 — Key Insight:**

AURA七个不变量与儒家五常（仁义礼智信）之间的结构收敛，并非偶然。这两个体系独立地发现了相同的治理架构要求——这一收敛本身就是证据，证明这些约束条件描述了真实的治理规律，而非文化偏好。

The structural convergence between AURA's seven invariants and the Confucian
Five Virtues (仁义礼智信) is not coincidental. Both systems independently arrived
at the same governance architecture requirements. This convergence is itself evidence
that these constraints describe real governance laws, not cultural preferences.

### 3. LAMAGUE — 跨文化治理语法 (Kuà Wénhuà Zhìlǐ Yǔfǎ)

LAMAGUE（通用伦理对齐、测量和治理语言）是一种正式语法，用于在不同传统之间编码治理义务，同时不抹去每种传统的独特性。

LAMAGUE is a formal grammar for encoding governance obligations across traditions
without erasing the distinctiveness of each.

**五常的LAMAGUE编码示例 — Example LAMAGUE Encoding of the Five Virtues:**

```
仁 rén (Benevolence):
  ⇌_bidirectional         — operates between people, not within one person alone
  + ✧_collective-flourishing — oriented toward shared flourishing
  + Φ↑_earned-through-care  — accumulated through demonstrated care over time
  + Ψ_consequence-aware     — must consider downstream effects
  + Ω_human-primacy         — the human relationship is primary

义 yì (Righteousness):
  ⊕_contextual-judgment    — determined by situation, not fixed rule
  + Δ_role-appropriate      — what is right varies by relational role
  + ♦_proportional-response — response proportionate to situation

礼 lǐ (Ritual Propriety):
  ⊞_structural-relationship — the form of relationship IS the governance
  + ⟲_recursive-maintenance  — continuously maintained, not achieved once
  + |◁▷|_non-negotiable      — the structures are constitutional, not optional
```

**儒家治理的关键洞见 — Key Insight from Confucian Governance:**

礼（lǐ）将关系结构本身作为治理的实质，而非其形式。这与西方程序性公正模型形成对比——在西方模型中，程序保护权利；而在儒家模型中，正确构建的关系本身就是治理。

礼 treats relational structure itself as the substance of governance, not its form.
This contrasts with Western procedural justice models — where procedure protects rights —
and with Māori kaitiakitanga — where ongoing relationship IS the governance.
All three arrive at relationship as central; they differ in how that relationship is structured.

## 新西兰人工智能治理标准 — NZ AI Governance Standards

面向中国政策制定者和研究者，以下是荔枝豹框架为新西兰开发的四项AI问责标准：

For Chinese policymakers and researchers, the following four AI accountability
standards have been developed for New Zealand's context:

### AI适格证书 (AI WOF) — Community AI Warrant of Fitness

新西兰的机动车"适格证书"（WOF）是全民认可的安全检查机制。荔枝豹框架将这一概念应用于AI系统：每年七项检查，通过/不通过，公开注册。

New Zealand's vehicle Warrant of Fitness is a nationally understood safety mechanism.
The Lycheetah Framework applies this concept to AI systems: seven annual checks,
pass/fail, public register.

**与中国AI治理的比较 — Comparison with Chinese AI Governance:**

中国的《互联网信息服务算法推荐管理规定》（2022年）要求算法透明度和用户权利保护。AI适格证书在结构上是互补的，但增加了：知识声明诚实性、实际(而非文件中的)人工覆盖、以及基于社区的申诉程序。

China's Algorithm Recommendation Regulations (2022) require algorithmic transparency
and user rights protection. The AI WOF is structurally complementary but adds:
honesty about knowledge claims, actually-functional (not just documented) human override,
and community-accessible appeal processes.

### 知识世界披露标准 — Three Worlds Disclosure Standard

基于毛利认识论：
- 光明世界 (Te Ao Mārama) — 系统高度确定的内容
- 夜晚世界 (Te Ao Pō) — 系统不确定的内容
- 潜能世界 (Te Kore) — 系统结构上无法知道的内容

Based on Māori epistemology:
- Te Ao Mārama (World of Light) — what the system knows with high confidence
- Te Ao Pō (World of Night) — what the system is uncertain about
- Te Kore (World of Potential) — what the system structurally cannot know

**对中国研究者的注释 — Note for Chinese Researchers:**

Te Kore（潜能世界）的概念——结构性的不可知性，而非暂时的无知——与道家的"道可道，非常道"有深刻的平行关系。真正的知识承认其自身的边界。荔枝豹框架认为这是一种AI设计要求，而非仅仅是哲学观点。

Te Kore (the world of potential/void) — structural unknowability as distinct from
temporary ignorance — has a deep parallel with the Daoist 道可道，非常道
(The Dao that can be named is not the eternal Dao). True knowledge acknowledges
its own limits. The Lycheetah Framework proposes this as an AI design requirement,
not merely a philosophical observation.
