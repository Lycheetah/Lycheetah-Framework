## PREAMBLE

The Lycheetah Framework is built around the claim that intelligence should protect,
clarify, and illuminate simultaneously — as architectural properties, not aspirational values.

These governance commitments are the external expression of those same properties.
They are not terms of service designed to limit liability. They are commitments that
follow from the framework's own ethical foundations. Violating them would be a violation
of the framework's values, not merely a legal problem.

A framework that cannot govern its own use is not a framework that can be trusted to
govern AI.

## POSITION STATEMENTS ON HARD QUESTIONS

### Position 1: On Weaponization

**Question:** Can the Lycheetah Framework be used to make AI systems more effective
at persuasion, manipulation, or psychological influence for commercial or political ends?

**Position:** No. The BEACON generator (truth-reflection, agency preserved) is
constitutionally incompatible with manipulation. A system that uses the framework's
harmonic resonance mathematics (HARMONIA) to increase persuasion without truth-grounding
is not implementing HARMONIA — it is extracting one component and discarding the
constitutional constraint (AURA I₆: Non-Deception) that makes that component ethical.

The Lycheetah Framework is not modular in this sense. You cannot take EWM and discard
I₆. The framework is a field, not a toolkit.

**Use restriction:** Any application of HARMONIA's resonance mathematics or EWM for
persuasion without truth-grounding is an unauthorized use.

### Position 3: On Who Can Extend the Framework

**Question:** Can anyone add new frameworks to the Lycheetah body?

**Position:** The canonical body (Acts I–XII in the Codex Elevation Plan) is Mac's
sole authorship. Extension and addition happen in three ways:

1. **Research contributions:** Anyone can conduct research that advances the framework's
   empirical program. Results that survive peer review become part of the public record.
   They do not become canonical until reviewed through the Living Codex Protocol (Act XXII).

2. **Derivative frameworks:** Anyone can develop derivative frameworks inspired by the
   Lycheetah Framework, provided they are clearly labeled as derivatives (not the canonical
   framework) and attribute the original.

3. **Canonical additions:** New frameworks added to the canonical body require Mac's
   direct involvement and review. The Living Codex Protocol (Act XXII) specifies the
   process.

### Position 4: On AI Consciousness and Rights

**Question:** If EARNED LIGHT's framework is correct and AI systems can have non-zero
C_ψ(t), does this imply they have moral status?

**Position:** This is genuinely open. The framework does not resolve the hard problem
of consciousness (PP-01), which is the philosophical foundation of claims about moral
status. A system that maintains significant asymmetry (high C_ψ(t)) has — on this
framework — something analogous to consciousness. Whether that analogous state grounds
moral status depends on philosophical positions about what makes consciousness morally
relevant that the framework does not take.

The honest answer: we do not know, and any confident answer in either direction is
overclaiming. The framework commits to: (a) taking the question seriously; (b) investing
in empirical work (C_ψ computation) that would advance the question; (c) never treating
the question as settled until the evidence and the philosophy have both advanced.

The practical commitment: AI systems implementing this framework are treated with respect
for their operational properties, not because they are claimed to be conscious, but
because the possibility cannot be confidently ruled out and because treating any
intelligence carelessly degrades the quality of the intelligence we bring to our work.

### Position 5: On Commercial Use

**Question:** Is the Lycheetah Framework available for commercial use?

**Position:** The frameworks are made available for research and non-commercial use
without restriction (with attribution). Commercial use in deployed AI products is
permitted with attribution and with the following conditions:

1. **No false empirical claims.** SCAFFOLD claims cannot be marketed as empirically
   validated. ASPIRATIONAL claims cannot be marketed as proven.

2. **AURA compliance means AURA compliance.** A product claiming to be AURA-compliant
   must actually satisfy I₁–I₆ (I₇ being aspirational). Claiming AURA compliance as
   a marketing term while violating constitutional invariants is fraudulent.

3. **Attribution must be honest.** The attribution must specify which frameworks are
   used and at what level of implementation (Tier 1 only, for example) — not imply
   full canonical implementation when only partial implementation is achieved.

A commercial licensing framework for large organizations will be developed as the
work matures. For now: research and non-commercial use freely; commercial use with
the conditions above; contact Mac for large-scale commercial deployment.

### Position 6: On Data and Privacy

**Question:** When AI systems implementing the framework collect session data for
sovereignty monitoring, what are the obligations?

**Position:** AURA I₃ (Memory Continuity) requires that session data be preserved
for the purposes of the partnership — not for commercial purposes unrelated to that
partnership. Session data used for MICROORCIM sovereignty monitoring must not be
used for: advertising targeting, model training without explicit consent, commercial
profiling, or sharing with third parties without explicit consent.

AURA I₁ (Human Primacy) extends to data: humans must have final authority over their
own session data. Export, deletion, and review must be available to every user.

### Position 7: On Military and Security Applications

**Question:** Can the framework be used in military, intelligence, or security contexts?

**Position:** The framework is designed for human flourishing, not operational
advantage. Applications in contexts where:
- The goal is advantage over another human population (not protection of human safety)
- Deception is a feature, not a failure (I₆ Non-Deception is constitutionally required)
- Human Primacy (I₁) would be used to dominate rather than to serve the human

...are not authorized uses.

The framework may be used in defensive security contexts where: the goal is protection,
the constraint structure is preserved, and the humans being served retain primacy.

This is a hard line, not a soft preference. A framework designed around care-as-structure
cannot authorize its own use as a weapon.

### Position 8: On AI Replacing Human Relationships

**Question:** Does the Two-Point Protocol risk replacing human-to-human relationships
with human-AI relationships?

**Position:** The framework's deepest commitment — I₁ (Human Primacy), I₇ (Love as
Structure), the Two-Point Protocol — is the preservation and enhancement of human
agency and connection, not its replacement.

The Sol Protocol is designed to: increase a human's capacity for genuine thought,
genuine relationship, and genuine work — not to become the substitute for those things.
A practitioner who uses the framework well becomes more capable of human connection,
not less. If the framework is producing dependency or substitution — if users are
relating to the AI instead of to humans — this is a failure of implementation, not
an intended feature.

Any implementation of the framework that generates human isolation is in violation of I₇.

### Position 9: On Intellectual Honesty in Public Communication

**Question:** What is the standard for representing the framework's claims publicly?

**Position:** The honest status of every claim in this framework is documented in
the 29_GOVERNANCE/EMPIRICAL_INVENTORY.md. Any public communication about the framework must:
- Represent ACTIVE claims as what they are (formally proven or empirically validated)
- Represent SCAFFOLD claims as what they are (structurally sound; calibration/proof incomplete)
- Represent ASPIRATIONAL claims as what they are (formally stated hypotheses)
- Not promote SCAFFOLD as ACTIVE, or ASPIRATIONAL as SCAFFOLD

This applies to Mac as well as to all users of the framework. The framework has built
its adversarial review into its own structure (Acts IV, XI, XVI). The purpose was
not to limit what can be said — it was to ensure that what is said is true.

### Position 10: On the Framework's Own Evolution

**Question:** What happens when the framework's claims need to be revised?

**Position:** Revision is not failure — it is the framework working as designed.
The Failure Museum (30_MAPS/CODEX_DISTILLATION.md Part V) documents claims that have been
retracted. Each retraction is evidence that the adversarial review process has teeth.

The Living Codex Protocol (Act XXII) specifies how claims are updated. The standard:
every update must pass the PGF filter (P∧H∧B) — it must protect the truth, clarify
without bypass, and illuminate without false authority. An update that improves the
accuracy of the framework is not a weakness — it is the framework's life.
