**Location:** `01_CASCADE_L4/essentials.md`

**Claims in tension:**

**The problem:** A 100% accuracy claim cannot be simultaneously true and dependent on uncalibrated parameters. If k₁–k₄ are not set, accuracy is undefined, not 100%.

**Recommended resolution:** Change to: "The reorganization mechanism formally guarantees invariant preservation [ACTIVE]. Empirical accuracy on real knowledge corpora is [SCAFFOLD] pending k₁–k₄ calibration."

## Part 3 — Notation Drift

### Drift §1 — Ψ (uppercase): Consciousness Operator vs Fold Operation

**Collision:**
- TRIAD, LAMAGUE: Ψ = consciousness/awareness operator
- CHRYSOPOEIA: "Ψ (Fold)" in the Solve et Coagula duality (where Ψ means the Fold = Coagula operation)
- CHRYSOPOEIA: ψ (lowercase) = state vector (ψ_initial, ψ_final, ψ*)

**Impact:** High. A reader moving from TRIAD/LAMAGUE to CHRYSOPOEIA will interpret Ψ as "consciousness" when it means "Fold operation." The lowercase/uppercase distinction (ψ = state, Ψ = operator) partially resolves this, but Ψ (uppercase) is still used two different ways across frameworks.

**Resolution:** In CHRYSOPOEIA, rename the Fold operator to a distinct symbol (e.g., **Λ** for Coagula, **⚘** for Solve as already partially done). Remove Ψ from CHRYSOPOEIA's Solve et Coagula notation.
