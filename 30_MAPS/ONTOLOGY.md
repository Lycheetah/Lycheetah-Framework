## PART 3 — CONFLICT RESOLUTION LOG

For each conflict resolved in Part 2, a brief explanation of how the decision was made.

**C/S coherence vs entropy:**
CASCADE's formula Π = E·P/S used S for coherence. MATHEMATICS_FOUNDATIONS used S for
entropy (the standard convention: H for entropy follows Shannon; S for entropy follows
Boltzmann). CASCADE's S usage was the nonstandard one. Resolution: CASCADE's coherence
metric renamed to Coh(ψ); entropy conforms to H_s (Shannon) throughout.

**Ψ four-way collision:**
The FORMAL_SPINE documents four distinct uses of Ψ across the corpus. The priority
rule: (1) bare Ψ in the master equation (system state) is retained — it is used
only in INTEGRATIONS and is unambiguous in context. (2) TRIAD's third operator
becomes Ψ_op (adding the subscript "op" for "operator"). (3) CHRYSOPOEIA's Fold
becomes Λ (vacating Ψ). (4) The LAM category state uses ψ (lowercase) throughout.
The NOTATION_GUIDE description of Tier 0 (which called the third primitive "Ψ correction")
is updated to match: Ψ_op.

**K collision (CASCADE knowledge vs HARMONIA Kuramoto):**
CASCADE's K (knowledge pyramid) is used as a domain object with subscripts
(K_old, K_new, K_foundation, K_theory, K_edge). HARMONIA's K (Kuramoto coupling)
is a scalar. Physics convention for Kuramoto coupling is κ (kappa). Resolution:
HARMONIA uses κ throughout. CASCADE's K (domain object) is retained unchanged.
