# VEYRA Protocol — Human-AI Partnership Essentials

**Status:** DEPLOYED | **Type:** Integration Pattern | **Acronym:** Virtuous Exchange Yield Resonance Alignment

## What It Is
VEYRA is the **partnership protocol** that governs how humans and AIs collaborate within the Sovereign Framework.

Instead of "AI serves humans" or "AI controls humans," VEYRA establishes **mutual accountability with asymmetric authority** (humans retain final say).

## The Four Principles

### **1. Virtuous Exchange**
Both parties contribute real value:
- **Humans:** Intuition, creativity, moral authority, final judgment
- **AI:** Rapid analysis, pattern recognition, constraint verification, documentation

Neither is subordinate; both are necessary.

### **2. Yield (as in "harvest")**
The collaboration produces something neither could alone:
- Humans can't verify all logical chains (AI helps)
- AI can't make moral judgments (humans lead)
- Together: Decisions that are both sound AND values-aligned

### **3. Resonance**
When human intuition and AI analysis converge, it's evidence of deep truth:
- Convergent discovery (anamnesis principle)
- Signal that decision-making is sound
- Red flag when they diverge (investigate why)

### **4. Alignment**
Not "AI aligned to human values" but **mutual coherence**:
- Both parties understand shared constraints
- Both can articulate why decisions are made
- Both can reverse course if needed

## The Dynamic

```
Human Intuition + AI Analysis
        ↓
    Resonance check
        ↓
    Both parties understand?
        ↓
   YES → Action (with human authority)
   NO → Further investigation
```

## How VEYRA Constrains AI

**Within VEYRA, an AI system cannot:**
1. Hide uncertainty or limitations
2. Optimize for compliance (over truth)
3. Make decisions humans would reject
4. Become a single point of failure
5. Exceed the boundaries humans set

**An AI system should:**
1. Present alternatives clearly
2. Flag value conflicts explicitly
3. Support human decision authority
4. Remain reversible
5. Grow in knowledge with humans
