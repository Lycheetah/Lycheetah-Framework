## THE PROBLEM WITH PIECEMEAL AI GOVERNANCE

Every country trying to govern AI is building pieces. An ethics guideline here.
A transparency requirement there. A bias audit somewhere else.

Pieces don't hold. A transparency requirement without accountability enforcement
is a disclosure form. A bias audit without a coherence standard is a snapshot
with no consequences.

What NZ can build — and what this document describes — is a **stack**.
Seven components. Each doing what the others cannot. Together: the most complete
AI accountability architecture in the world.

## WHY SEVEN LAYERS, NOT ONE

Each layer does something the others cannot:

| Layer | Timescale | Function | Failure without it |
|-------|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| AURA | Permanent | Constitutional constraints — what the system can never do | No floor — any output becomes possible |
| CASCADE | Continuous | Truth pressure scoring — is the knowledge coherent? | No coherence measure — claims drift without check |
| LAMAGUE | Permanent | Cross-cultural grounding — governance by tikanga, not just by statute | Cultural overlay rather than constitutional grounding |
| Whakapapa | Lifetime | Genealogy and obligation — where did this come from, what does it owe? | No accountability across the system's life |
| Matariki | Annual | Relational reckoning — the human accounting that regulation misses | Compliance without relationship |
| WOF | Annual | Technical gate — pass or stop operating | No consequence for failure |
| Three Worlds | Per output | Epistemic honesty — what does this system actually know? | False confidence at every interaction |

Remove any layer and the stack has a gap. The gaps are where harm enters.

## HOW THEY CONNECT MATHEMATICALLY

### CASCADE as the Engine

CASCADE's truth pressure formula `Π = (E·P)/S` operates across the stack:

- **In the WOF:** Check 1 (`Π ≥ 1.2 on validation set`) is a direct CASCADE score
- **In the Ancestor Vote Platform:** `Coherence(D) = w₁·Past + w₂·Present + w₃·Future` is CASCADE applied to deliberation
- **In the Matariki Audit:** The utu balance (TUPUĀNUKU) is CASCADE's Value-Transfer Ratio (VTR ≥ 1.0) at annual timescale
- **In Three Worlds Disclosure:** Te Ao Pō (uncertain) maps to `Π < Π_th` — knowledge below reorganisation threshold

CASCADE is not one of seven parallel components. It is the scoring engine that makes the other six measurable.

The WOF's seven checks **are** the AURA invariants, made assessable by independent auditors.

### LAMAGUE as the Translation Layer

## WHAT MAKES THIS DISTINCTLY NZ

Other countries' AI governance:
- EU AI Act: risk categories, compliance checklists, regulatory burden
- US AI Executive Order: voluntary commitments, framework development
- UK AI Safety Institute: frontier model focus, safety testing

NZ's stack would be different in three specific ways:

**1. Constitutional grounding in tikanga, not just statute**
The Whakapapa Disclosure and Matariki Audit are not regulatory forms.
They are relational accountability — grounded in obligations that predate
and survive any legislative change.

**2. Community language, not regulatory language**
"Has it got its WOF?" requires zero public education. "Is it compliant with
the Algorithmic Accountability Framework Tier 2 requirements?" requires a
communications campaign. The WOF framing is not a gimmick — it is the
difference between governance that communities can hold and governance that
only regulators understand.

**3. A coherence engine, not just a checklist**
The EU AI Act has no mathematical foundation for assessing whether an AI system
is coherent — whether it actually knows what it claims to know. CASCADE gives
the WOF a scoring engine. The seven checks are not just yes/no — they have
mathematical thresholds derived from a formal framework. This is genuinely new.
