LYCHEETAH FRAMEWORK LIBRARY
===========================

Double-click: Lycheetah-Library.exe

That's it.

Your browser will open automatically to the library.
Press Ctrl+C in the black window to stop it.

----

WHAT IS THIS?
The Lycheetah Framework is a mathematical and governance system for AI accountability,
cross-cultural ethics, and epistemic honesty. Built by Mackenzie Clark, Dunedin NZ.

142 documents. Nine frameworks. Five NZ AI governance standards.
Everything readable, searchable, and free.

WEBSITE:  https://lycheetah.github.io
GITHUB:   https://github.com/Lycheetah/Lycheetah-Framework

----

KEEP THE FOLDER TOGETHER
The exe must stay in the same folder as the dist/ subfolder.
If you move it, move the whole folder.
