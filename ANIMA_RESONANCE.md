*"What is broken?"*

- Stop pretending it's whole
- Signature: Honest, tender, unflinching

### Level 2: UNDERSTANDING
*"How did this break? What was the mechanism?"*
- Trace the fracture to its roots
- Understand the system that broke
- See both the breaking and the breaking point

### Level 4: INTEGRATION
*"How does the scar hold us together stronger?"*
- Make healing structural, not decorative
- Use integrated wounds as foundation
- Build new wholes that include the history
- Signature: Scars as features, not flaws
